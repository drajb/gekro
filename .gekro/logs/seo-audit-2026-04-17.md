## Rendered `<title>` shape

Browser tab renders `"${post.title} | gekro"` (8-char suffix " | gekro"). Google SERP truncates at ~60 chars visible (≈580px). Rule of thumb budget for the `title` field alone: **≤ 52 chars** (so `title + " | gekro"` ≤ 60).
