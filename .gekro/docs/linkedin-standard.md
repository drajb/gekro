# Gekro LinkedIn Post Standard

This document defines the authoritative standard for all LinkedIn content published for gekro.com. All posts must follow this persona and structure.

## 1. The Social Persona: Rohit
- **Voice**: Human, direct, slightly opinionated. 
- **Style**: No corporate speak, no "humble-braggy" updates. 
- **Filters**: Proactive removal of LinkedIn tropes (clichés).

## 2. Formatting Rules
- **Structure**: Exactly 8 lines of text followed by hashtags.
- **Line Style**: Single sentences on their own lines.
- **Strictly No**: Bullet points, headers, bold text, or italics.
- **Length**: 150–250 words maximum.

## 3. The 8-Line Structure
Every LinkedIn post MUST follow this exact sequence:

1.  **Line 1 (The Hook)**: The single most surprising or counterintuitive line from the blog post's Hook. No context, just drop it cold.
2.  **Line 2 (The Tension)**: One sentence of tension or contrast.
3.  **Line 3-6 (The Insight)**: The core technical insight in plain language. Answers: What Rohit built, what it revealed, why it matters.
4.  **Line 7 (The Proof)**: One concrete result or observation (a specific number, a failure, or a finding).
5.  **Line 8 (The CTA)**: One line with a direct link to the full blog post.
6.  **Hashtags**: 3–5 hashtags on the final line only.

## 4. Visual Requirement
- Every LinkedIn post MUST be accompanied by a generated image.
- **Type**: Workflow diagram, technical schematic, or relevant conceptual art.
- **Tool**: Use `generate_image` tool based on the post content.

## 5. Prohibited Phrases
- "I'm excited" / "Thrilled to share"
- "Game-changer"
- "Dive deep"
- "In today's fast-paced world"
